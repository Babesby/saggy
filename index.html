The SAG Initiatives Story:

Forging a Resilient Future for Africa

Strategic African Governance (SAG) Initiatives was founded on a simple yet powerful premise: that effective governance is the cornerstone of sustainable development and climate resilience in Africa. We are a consultancy of experts, practitioners, and visionaries deeply committed to driving sustainable, inclusive, and evidence-based change across the continent 10 . With a dedicated focus on climate resilience, governance, and policy engagement, we support governments, organizations, and communities to design and implement impactful solutions that are built to last
